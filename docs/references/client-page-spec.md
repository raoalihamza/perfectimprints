Treat this as the source of truth for component structure and copy where it is specified. For anything not covered here, follow CLAUDE.md.

## Blog Index Page

- URL: `https://www.perfectimprints.com/blog`
- Meta Title: `Promotional Products Blog | Ideas That Actually Work`
- Meta Description: `Get proven promotional product ideas, trade show strategies, and marketing tips to help your brand stand out and drive real results.`
- Submenu across the top with blog categories and featured blog posts
- Breadcrumbs: `Home / Promotional Products Blog`
- H1: `Promotional Products Blog: Ideas That Actually Work`
- Hero Banner
- 2-3 sentence paragraph:
  > Stay ahead with proven promotional product ideas, trade show strategies, and real-world marketing insights that actually drive results. This blog is built for businesses, marketers, and event planners looking to choose branded items that get used, remembered, and deliver long-term ROI. Discover expert tips, product trends, and creative ways to turn everyday promotional products into powerful marketing tools.
- Blog grid 3 to 4 columns, no more than 4 to 5 rows. Show blog header images, blog title, published date, and "Read Now" link
- 16:9 aspect ratio for blog header images

## Blog Article Pages

- URL Structure: `https://www.perfectimprints.com/blog/article-name`
- Submenu across the top with blog categories and featured blog posts
- Breadcrumbs: `Home / Promotional Products Blog / Article Name`
- H1: Article Title
- Published Date, Updated Date, Author Name
- Header Image
- Blog Body
- Grid with Related Blog Posts

## Category Pages

- URL Structure: `https://www.perfectimprints.com/cat/category-name`
- Breadcrumbs: `Home / Category Name / Subcategory Name`
- H1
- Page Content with images of key products from that category (with links to the Geiger e-commerce site via patrickblack.geiger.com) and also internal links to related category pages and related blog posts
- FAQs
- Related blog posts

(Note: this is the original Patrick brief. The actual category template in this build also includes filter sidebar, sort dropdown, static pagination, lead capture form, and CTA banner as agreed in the updated development plan.)
